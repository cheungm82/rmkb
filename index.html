About Us

RMKB Ventures takes on the big challenges with you. We get excited at the idea of working with talented founders to co-create technology and impact. Being entrepreneurs ourselves, we get that it is hard to bring a company to life: the daily struggles, the roadblocks and even the occasional rout.

That is why RMKB walks the journey alongside you, together. We bring what we know about the space and who we know in the landscape to navigate around problems, open doors and take your startup to the next level. We believe this makes all the difference.

  • CEO / Founder : Kevin Osmond
    Founded : 2012
    Website : www.printerous.com

    Established in 2012, Printerous is an online platform producing the finest quality of printed goods on-demand. Printerous allows their customers to convert their personal digital photo collecitions or artwork into customized keepsakes. Customers can print Photo Books, Photo Prints, Canvas Arts, Tote Bags, Throw Pillows, Magnets, Gadget Cases and more in just a few clicks.

    Printerous also features a curated marketplace that allows their customers to look for products designed by designers, illustrators and artists from all over Indonesia. This marketplace provides artists with an additional source of income without any additonal work and customers with unique products that cannot be acquired anywhere else.

    Printerous makes art easy.
